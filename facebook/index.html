Why should you advertise on social media?

Be where your audience is

Your audience, especially the youngest generations, spend the majority of their time on platforms such as Facebook and Instagram. You need to be reaching them where they already are, and show them that you have their best interests in mind.

Reach new students in an engaging way

Social Media Advertising allows you to target and interact with potential students in a way that isn’t possible on other platforms, such as lookalike audiences and lead-gen forms that let you keep the conversation going.

Deliver value and they will come to you

Our campaigns are primarily focused on lead generation, which means we exchange value for a student’s contact information. It’s a psychological rule that humans respond to goodwill with more goodwill (if done correctly). We design our campaigns around this, showing students that you really care.
